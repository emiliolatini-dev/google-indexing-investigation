## Purpose of this chapter

State only what the evidence supports: which findings are established, which
hypotheses remain open, and what the responsible next steps are. Conclusions must
not exceed the evidence. If the evidence does not settle the question, the
chapter says so explicitly. No accusatory or speculative framing.
