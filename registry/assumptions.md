# Assumptions Registry

> **Status:** empty — no assumptions recorded yet.

This registry holds every **ASSUMPTION** (`A-`): a premise the reasoning depends
on but does not itself evidence. Assumptions are not forbidden — they are made
**explicit** so they can be challenged, tested, and where possible converted into
facts.

Canonical definition and the rule for when an assumption must be documented:
[report/02-methodology.md §2.1](../report/02-methodology.md#21-assumption--definition-and-use).
